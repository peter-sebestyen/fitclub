What is FitClub?

FitClub is a Community with people who enjoy to workout together. It doesn´t matter if you are young or from another century, if you want to get (or stay) fit, you are welcome. We always have a lot of happiness in the group and we would love to share it with everybody!

What do you need?

You don´t need much to join us for a Workout. You only need:

  • A Fitness/Yoga Mat
  • A water bottle (maybe with electrolyts)
  • And MOTIVATION
